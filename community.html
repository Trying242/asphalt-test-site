Local asphalt services that keep El Paso moving

If you’re searching for asphalt paving, sealcoating, parking lot striping, line painting, or asphalt repair in El Paso, TX, this page explains how we help local properties stay safe and look professional.

Parking lot striping & line painting

Fresh parking lot striping improves traffic flow, visibility, and safety. We handle ADA stalls, arrows, fire lanes, loading zones, and custom stencils so your lot is easier to navigate—day or night.

Commercial asphalt paving

From new asphalt paving to overlays and expansions, we build surfaces designed for real traffic. Proper grading and compaction help reduce early cracking, standing water, and uneven wear.

Asphalt repair & maintenance

Pothole repair, crack sealing, patching, and surface repairs stop damage from spreading. Pair repairs with sealcoating to protect the lot and extend its life.

Why this matters for local businesses

A clean, clearly marked parking lot helps customers feel confident the moment they arrive. Good paving and striping also reduces trip hazards, improves ADA access, and can help lower liability risk.

Whether you need asphalt paving for a new build, asphalt repair for potholes and cracking, sealcoating for protection, or parking lot striping/line painting for a fresh layout—our goal is simple: deliver durable work with a professional finish.
