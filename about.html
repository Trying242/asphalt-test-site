About our asphalt company

Complete Parking Lot Solutions is a commercial asphalt contractor serving El Paso, Texas. We specialize in asphalt paving, sealcoating, asphalt repair, and parking lot striping/line painting—built around clear communication, clean job sites, and results that hold up under traffic.

What we do best
  • Commercial asphalt paving & overlays
  • Pothole repair, crack sealing, and patching
  • Sealcoating for protection and curb appeal
  • Parking lot striping, ADA, and fire lanes

Built for commercial properties

Our process

Every site is different. We start with a quick walkthrough to spot problem areas (drainage, alligator cracking, failed patches, potholes), then recommend a plan that matches your goals and budget.

1) Evaluate
Measure, photograph, and define scope.
2) Plan
Sequence work for traffic control and access.
3) Build
Prep, repair, pave/sealcoat, then stripe.
4) Maintain
Plan crack sealing and future sealcoats.
